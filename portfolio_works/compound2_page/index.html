STRATEGIES: OVERVIEW

The right investment strategy...
is the one that’s right for you!

Every investment strategy comes down to one thing—balance of risk & reward.
Ultimately, the best strategy is the one that fits your goals, desires, and experience
level. That's why CSE offers a variety of seminars, toolboxes and platinum services
to support a variety of strategies.

COVERED CALLS
Earn a monthly return of 3%-6% in as few as 4 hrs./month!

Trading covered calls options is a proven rule-based strategy with a proven success
record. For conservative investors, this strategy offers the least risk and, for both
experienced and novice traders, it's really the best place to start.
