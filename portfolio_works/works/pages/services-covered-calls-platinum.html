PLATINUM SERVICES: COVERED CALLS PLATINUM SERVICE

In addition to investor education seminars and software tools, Compound Stock Earnings (CSE) provides a subscription Platinum Service for each of CSE’s rules-based techniques. The Covered Call Platinum Service is run by Randy Bassett, who also teaches the Covered Call and Advanced Charting Seminars.

The Covered Call Platinum Service takes the theory learned in the 2-Day Covered Calls Seminar and puts it into practice. Subscribers receive on-going weekly education demonstrating how covered calls are selected, entered, managed and exited by following how Randy manages his own, personal portfolio.

Subscribers to the Covered Call Platinum Service receive email and smartphone updates of trades that Randy makes in his own account. Subscribers can then choose whether to enter the same or similar position or can monitor the positions over time to improve their covered call investing skills.

Covered Call Platinum Service subscribers can attend a weekly Webinar at 11am Central time each Wednesday morning where Randy reviews current positions and assesses possible trades for the coming week. These positions are analyzed during live market hours with thorough explanations of which positions may be suitable to trade. In the event you are unable to attend the webinar live, each webinar is recorded and available via archive so that you can view each week’s session at your convenience.

Overview

  • Provides an unprecedented level of Covered Call education
  • Investors can witness trades as they are being analyzed
  • Excellent real-world training for people new to CSE covered call techniques
  • Run by Randy Bassett, who teaches CSE Covered Call and Advanced Charting seminars
  • Weekly live Webinar during market hours
  • Trade alerts are sent by email and smartphone

Subscribers of this service receive...

  • Attendance at the weekly two-hour live Covered Call Platinum Service Webinar
  • Access to recorded version of the weekly Webinar
  • Email and smartphone alerts with actual trade selections (including strike price, expiration, execution price and portfolio allocation) that we are executing in our own accounts.
  • Free attendance to the 2-Day Covered Calls Seminar
  • Email support for any questions you may have on the service or selected positions
  • Free access to the CSE Toolbox, including:
    • CSE Covered Calls Screener
    • Option Chain
    • Automated SSR Worksheet
    • Stretched SSR Worksheet
    • Automated CPR Worksheet
    • Stock Researcher
    • TSS Strike Price Selector
    • Basic Charting Tool
