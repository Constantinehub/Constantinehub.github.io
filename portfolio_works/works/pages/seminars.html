2-Day Credit Spread/Strangle, Straddle, Hold (SSH) Seminar

  • Covers the basics of Credit Spreads and Strangle, Straddle, Hold (SSH) trading
  • Credit Spread (Saturday) Session... provides an understanding of: the various credit spread techniques, use of the CSE Toolbox Credit Spread Plug-in, basic line chart assessment, and capital preservation
  • Strangle, Straddle, Hold (Sunday) Session... provides an understanding of: why the SSH strategy is effective, an understanding of the different techniques, how to search for the best SSH opportunities, how to use SSH strategies in combination with other strategies, how to properly maintain a position
  • Online Saturday/Sunday sessions, offered several times throughout the year; allows flexibility to attend from your own home
