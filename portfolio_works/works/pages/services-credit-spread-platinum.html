PLATINUM SERVICES: CREDIT SPREAD PLATINUM SERVICE

In addition to investor education seminars and software tools, Compound Stock Earnings provides a subscription Platinum Service for each of CSE’s rules-based techniques. The Weekly Credit Spread Platinum Service is run by Keith Frampton, who also teaches the Credit Spread/SSH Seminar.

The Credit Spread Platinum Service focuses on weekly credit spreads, which allows four times the activity versus traditional monthly credit spreads.

Weekly Credit Spread Platinum Service subscribers can attend a weekly Webinar at 11am Central time each Tuesday morning where Keith reviews the previous week’s trades and assess possible trades for the coming week. These positions are analyzed during live market hours with thorough explanations of which positions may be suitable to trade. In the event you are unable to attend the webinar live, each webinar is recorded and available via archive so that you can view each week’s session at your convenience.

Subscribers to the Weekly Credit Spread Platinum Service receive email and smart phone updates of trades that Keith makes in his own account. Subscribers can then choose whether to enter the same or similar position or can monitor the positions over time to improve their covered call investing skills.

Overview

  • Provides an unprecedented level of Credit Spread education and trade selection designed for conservative investors who want:
  • Precise guidance on exactly what position to enter and when
  • The ease and convenience of just one simple trade per week
  • The potential to generate 5% returns per week (selections have averaged 5% per week over the past 56 weeks); and
  • Daily coaching and education to understand the rules, rationale and logic behind selected positions

Subscribers of this service receive...

  • Email and smartphone alerts with actual trade selection (including strike price, expiration, execution price and portfolio allocation) that we are executing in our own accounts.
  • Follow-up emails if any subsequent management moves (which are rarely required) are needed on open positions.
  • Attendance at weekly webinars hosted each Tuesday at 11:00am CST. These webinars provide the logic and rules behind selected trades and any management moves and are integral to clients learning the practical application of the techniques.
  • Email support for any questions you may have on the service or selected positions.
  • Free attendance to the Credit Spread / SSH Seminar
  • Free access to the CSE Credit Spread Toolbox, including:
    • Stock Credit Spread Screener
    • ETF Credit Spread Screener
    • Index Credit Spread Screener
    • Probability Calculator
    • Option Chain
